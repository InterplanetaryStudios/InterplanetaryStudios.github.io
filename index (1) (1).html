// About Us

Who We Are

Interplanetary Studios is a small indie game development team making free browser games — no ads, no AI-generated content, no paywalls. Every pixel, line of code, and note of music is handcrafted.

We started with a bullet hell jam entry and just kept going. From plinko idle games to physics-destruction sandboxes, we build things we'd actually want to play and share them with anyone who wants to pick them up.

Got feedback or just want to say hi? Join the Discord — we're active and love hearing from players.
